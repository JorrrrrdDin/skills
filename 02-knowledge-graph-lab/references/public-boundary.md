# Public Boundary

This skill is intended to be useful as a public note-analysis tool. It is not intended as a new patent filing package or legal freedom-to-operate opinion.

## Safe To Disclose

- The tool analyzes Markdown/text corpora.
- It computes practical scores for centrality, linkage, recency, text quality, and review priority.
- It identifies center notes, orphan notes, possible noise, and overgrown groups.
- It can be used on Obsidian vaults, research notes, paper cards, invention notes, and memory exports.
- It is experimental and heuristic.
- It may flag possible contamination, but this means "needs review" rather than "is false."

## Avoid In Public Claims

- Do not say the skill is legally clear of existing rights.
- Do not say it implements any protected internal system.
- Do not claim guaranteed detection, guaranteed cleanup, or academic validation.
- Do not publish private benchmark claims unless the backing artifacts are included.

## Good Public Framing

"Knowledge Gravity Lab is an experimental AI skill for mapping research notes and memory corpora. It helps identify center topics, noisy notes, orphaned ideas, overgrown topic groups, and cleanup actions."

## Bad Public Framing

"This is legally clear and implements a complete protected control algorithm."

## Review Checklist

Before publishing:

- Remove private formulas.
- Remove unpublished claim language.
- Replace "detects bad data" with "flags possible review items."
- Replace "freedom to operate" with "not assessed; needs legal review if commercialized."
- Replace "proves" with "suggests" unless backed by a saved experiment.
- Include experimental status.
